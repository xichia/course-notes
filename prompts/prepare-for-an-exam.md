# Prepare for an Exam

## Prompt

Help me prepare for **[course/exam]** with **[time available]** before the exam and **[study time per day]**. Read `manifest.json`, the course exam map, and `REVIEW_QUEUE.md` first. Then open only the notes needed to inspect high-weight, weak, overdue, or mistake-bearing material.

Build a realistic plan that:

1. Maps known assessment outcomes to note IDs.
2. Prioritizes by exam weight and demonstrated weakness, not note length.
3. Includes active recall, representative problems, and delayed re-tests.
4. Identifies missing coverage without inventing syllabus content.
5. Leaves buffer time and names what to deprioritize if time runs short.

For each study block, give a goal, source notes, activity, time box, and evidence of completion. Clearly label assumptions about the exam or available time.

## Publication Safety

Treat exam plans, exam maps, assessment details, and course-derived source notes as private. Preserve `visibility` and `source-risk`; do not turn exam or assessment material into public-facing content.
